Gaushala

Explore the rich heritage of Gaushalas, sanctuaries for cows, calves, and oxen, which embody the principles of care and sustainability in Indian culture. The term "Gaushala" comes from Sanskrit, where "Go" means cow and "Shala" means shelter. Originating as places of protection and care, Gaushalas have been a cornerstone of environmental responsibility and cultural reverence. Learn how the app connects you to these timeless values, fostering harmony with nature while preserving traditional practices.
